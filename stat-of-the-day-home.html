Tyrese Haliburton's scorching shooting in the clutch




Tyrese Haliburton has demonstrated exceptional proficiency in clutch situations throughout the current season, boasting an impressive shooting accuracy of 73.7% from the field, 77.8% from beyond the arc, and a flawless 100% success rate at the free-throw line. His remarkable composure and precision during critical moments highlight his ability to deliver under pressure, making him a key asset for his Indiana Pacers when the game hangs in the balance.
